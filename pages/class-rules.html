Class Rules

Grading Policy

  • Lab Demo 13%
    • Please finish the demo on the lab due time in the lab or schedule a time slot at TA hour before the following lab.
    • After one week, no lab demo will be accepted.
    • Each student can only use TA hours to demo for 3 times.
    • No delay is allowed.
  • Lab report 7%
    • For every lab, please submit a result report.
    • Submit to EEclass: lab results/screen shots and discussion.
    • Before the following lab session.
    • No delay is allowed.
  • Homework 20%
    • If delayed within one week after due, the score will get 20% discount.
    • After one week no credit will be given.
  • Exam 30%
    • Based on lab and homework
  • Final Project/Exam 30%
  • We may add bonus to final scores.

Rules of Lab Activities

  • Sign in and sign out at each lab
  • No food or drink in the lab.
  • TA should only help you understand the issue, but TA should not do the lab for you. If TA or other students do a lab for you, you will get a deduction on your lab credit.
  • TAs will ask you questions for each lab. Your answer will be evaluated for lab credit.
  • The lab be closed at 6:20pm. All students should leave the lab by 6:50pm.
  • You will lose lab credit without taking advice or instructions from TA or teacher.
  • If you can't finish the lab on time, you need to demo it in the next weekâ€™s lab session.

Rules of Leave of Absence

  • Students can take a leave of absence if following conditions hold
    • On school's service with official proof
    • On doctor's order with sickness
  • The students can ask TAs for extra time to finish the lab for above reasons only.

Rules of lab equipments

  • You should check the lab equipment/material with TAs before sign in and after sign out
  • Your grade might get deducted for careless damage or lost.
  • For damages to material, your final grade will be downgraded one level.

Rules of Punishment

  • Deduct 0.5 point for each violation of the following
    • Late for lab or early leave from lab sessions
    • Eat or drink anything except water in the lab
    • Ask other students or TA to perform your lab work
    • Fail to finish a lab on time
  • Deduct 1 point for each violation of the following
    • Fail to submit a lab preview report
    • Missing a homework
    • Break or lose lab equipment (serious damage will degrade final grade)
    • Absent from the lab without a proper reason
  • Deduct 2 point for each violation of the following
    • Any dishonest activities (plagiarism)
    • Copy other's codes
    • Copy other's homework
    • Give your codes to other students
    • Give your homework to other students
  • For three points of violation, a student will fail this course (grade X). Please withdraw this course.
  • With >= 1 point deduction, a student is not qualified for any bonus for final grades.
